Celebrating the Life of Rudolph Drake

A life well Lived

September 14, 1939 - February 6, 2026

New Testament Church of God - St Ann's Bay April 04, 2026 @ 11:00 AM

RUDOLPH “BOWLA” DRAKE

Written by BUTTA – March 17, 2026

Real times we have shared and spent together.

Under your shed, just like son and father.

Doing what we do with much fun and laughter.

Oh, the memories are ever etched and cannot falter.

Little things and big things, you were indeed a teacher.

Pops, or Bully or Bowla, you will always be remembered.

Having a sip or licking a cup, everything will be treasured.

Deep within, the memories fill my cup like a big jar:

Recall you driving us to high school in Brown’s town, daily, so far.

And in music, you were a promoter, singer and a real star.

King of the hills, you were and you still are.

Every day, you will be missed, you were a real spar.

— BUTTA
